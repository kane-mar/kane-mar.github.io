### 👤 About Me

Hi, I'm **Kane**. I am a software developer, automation specialist, and technical trainer focused on building modular, privacy-first software architectures. I specialize in TypeScript development, containerized microservices, and orchestrating robust, automated data pipelines.

My architectural philosophy leans heavily toward **local-first computing**. I believe in decentralizing data infrastructure, leveraging on-device LLMs, and minimizing reliance on heavy, proprietary cloud frameworks to build systems that are fast, private, and highly cost-effective. Although I do think Deepseek-v4-flash to be pretty awesome!

Beyond writing code, I consult and train organizations on agile execution. I advocate strongly for keeping technical engineering practices at the center of product delivery (and a healthy skepticism toward tool-enforced bureaucratic overhead). 

My [website](https://AgileFederation.com) is AgileFederation.com. And, I have a [substack](https://agilefederation.substack.com) at where I write about system dynamics and strategic foresight, more specifically, about organisational change and the impact of AI on software organisations.
